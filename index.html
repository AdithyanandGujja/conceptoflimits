What Is a Limit?

Imagine you are walking toward a wall, but you can never actually touch it. You take a big step, then a smaller step, then a tiny, tiny step. Each time, you get closer and closer to the wall, but you never quite get there.

A limit is just the special spot you get super close to, even if you never actually get there!
